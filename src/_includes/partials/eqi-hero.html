A little about myself

Hi, I'm John and I’m from the North East of England. I am married to my Swedish wife Lisa and we have three sons, Ben, Jack and Jonas.

For the last thirty years I have been a leader in a church setting as the Lead Pastor of a church, involved in leading teams and hundreds of people through change and helping them in their journey.

This journey included navigating the construction of a £5 million church building and conference centre in the middle of a Global financial crisis and leading a church through challenging seasons and transitions.

I'm still a Pastor!

As an associate Pastor at Glow Church Durham I have the privilege of serving the vision there, and I am also a Coach using EQi tools and an experienced speaker.
